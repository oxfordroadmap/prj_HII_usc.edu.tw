# Settlement and Investigation Framework

## Objective
To mitigate ongoing international reputational risks to Shih Chien University, restore research integrity within the Creative Industries Doctoral Program, and provide a transparent resolution framework prior to formal MoE administrative adjudication.

## Proposed Resolution Terms
1. **Fact-Finding Compliance**: Complete verification of the Respondent’s data provenance markers.
2. **MoE Accountability Alignment**: Align the institution's internal review timeline with the reporting standards required by the Ministry of Education (MoE).
