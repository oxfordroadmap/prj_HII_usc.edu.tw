# Evidence Repository: Access Control and Privacy Protection

## Purpose
This directory contains raw cryptographic artifacts, correspondence logs, and digital metadata. To protect individual privacy while serving public academic interests, direct access to raw files is restricted.

## Authorized Access Policy
In accordance with statutory petition procedures, full decrypted access to the raw data files is strictly limited to authorized investigators from the **Ministry of Education (MoE), Taiwan**, and the formal Institutional Research Ethics Committee.

## Verification Protocol
Third-party auditors may verify the integrity of any file by cross-checking its locally generated SHA-256 hash against the public [[_Evidence_Index.csv]].
