# Regulatory and Accountability Mapping

## Legal Obligations (Administrative Procedure Act & MoE Mandates)
* **Duty of Fact-Finding (Section 36)**: Institutional duty to investigate evidence ex officio. Failure to comply triggers MoE administrative remedy protocols.
* **Petitions Handling (教育部處理人民陳情案件作業規定)**: Establishes the exact timeline and qualitative standards that the target institution must maintain when responding to MoE-escalated cases.
* **Duty of Conflict Mitigation (Section 32/33)**: Personnel recusal requirements to prevent procedural contamination.

## Research Lifecycle Management (RCM) Obligations
* **Data Provenance**: Obligation to maintain verifiable digital timestamps for all project applications.
* **Authorship Integrity**: Obligation to record exact timelines of authorship assignment or waiver.
