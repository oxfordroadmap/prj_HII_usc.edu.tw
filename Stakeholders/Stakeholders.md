# Stakeholders: Roles and Capacities

## Regulatory & Supervisory Authority (External) 
* **Official Role**: Ministry of Education, Taiwan (中華民國教育部高教司) 
* **Scope of Duty**: Final arbiter of academic administrative compliance, oversight of university governance, and processing of public petitions/complaints under statutory regulations.

## Institutional Governance (High-Level)
* **Official Role**: Executive Management (校方最高決策層)
* **Scope of Duty**: Overall institutional compliance and research integrity oversight.

## Departmental Management
* **Official Role**: Program Director (博班所長/主任)
* **Scope of Duty**: Program-level academic supervision and first-line dispute assessment.

## Administrative Audit
* **Official Role**: R&D Office  (研發處)
* **Scope of Duty**: Procedural compliance, timeline enforcement, and official intake of misconduct reports.

## Research Execution
* **Official Role**: Respondent / Student (被檢舉人/學生)
* * **Scope of Duty**: Adherence to Research Lifecycle Management (RCM) and data provenance integrity.
